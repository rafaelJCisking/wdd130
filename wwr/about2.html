We are a company that loves nature and outdoor sports. Join us on incredible adventures! At Rafting Trancura Chile, we specialize in your safety while you enjoy the fresh summer breeze of southern Chile.

History

The Trancura River, nestled in the heart of Chile's Lake District near Pucón, has been a premier destination for white-water rafting since the 1990s. Classified as Class III-IV rapids, it offers an exhilarating yet accessible challenge for adventurers worldwide.

Founded by local guides with decades of river experience, Rafting Trancura Chile began as a small family operation. Today, we proudly share our passion for the Andes, combining expert instruction with deep respect for the environment. Every rapid tells a story — and we're honored to be part of yours.
