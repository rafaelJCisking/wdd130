BIO-BIO

Bio-Bio is a state in the southcentral Chile. It is boarder by Argentine to the east, VII region del Maule to the north, IX region de la Araucania to the south and Pacific ocean to the west. The state is a center of education, research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor recreation. Concepcion is the state capital and largest city, is home to the Temple of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Days Saints (LDS Church).
